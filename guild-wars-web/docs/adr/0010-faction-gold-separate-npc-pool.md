Factions hold their own gold pool (Faction Gold, starts 100Gp), distinct from Guild Gold held in `guild.resources`. Guilds interact with factions via the shared trade queue rather than through a sub-account or ledger view.

This lets factions act as autonomous NPC economic agents — accumulating gold, buying inputs, selling outputs — independently of player decisions. The alternative (faction gold as a sub-account of the controlling guild) would have collapsed the NPC economy into the player economy and made the market unresponsive to player neglect.
