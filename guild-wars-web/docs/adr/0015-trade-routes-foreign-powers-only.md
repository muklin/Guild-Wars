Trade Routes can only originate from a Foreign Power. Terrain plots cannot be the origin of a Trade Route.

An earlier design allowed Trade Routes from terrain plots (representing small local trade), but this was dropped: terrain-plot trade would require a separate routing model, different scale mechanics, and additional UI. Keeping Trade Routes exclusive to Foreign Powers maintains a clean separation — local economic activity stays within the faction/district system, while Trade Routes represent substantial inter-civilisation commerce.
